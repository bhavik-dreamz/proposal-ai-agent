# AI Proposal Generator - Naming Suggestions

## Project Overview
AI-powered proposal generator for IT project managers that:
- Automatically generates professional project proposals using OpenAI GPT-4
- Analyzes client requirements
- Provides accurate cost and timeline estimates

## Top Name Recommendations

### 1. **ProposalForge AI** ⭐ (Recommended)
**Rationale**: 
- "Forge" implies crafting/building something strong and professional
- Clear indication of AI technology
- Memorable and professional
- Domain available: proposalforge.ai

### 2. **ProposiGPT**
**Rationale**:
- Combines "Proposal" with "GPT" (the underlying technology)
- Modern, tech-forward branding
- Easy to remember
- Clearly indicates AI-powered functionality

### 3. **BidCraft AI**
**Rationale**:
- "Bid" resonates with project managers submitting proposals
- "Craft" suggests quality and precision
- Professional and concise

### 4. **ProjectProposer**
**Rationale**:
- Straightforward and descriptive
- Easy to understand the purpose
- Professional naming convention

### 5. **EstiMate AI**
**Rationale**:
- Clever play on "Estimate" and "Mate" (assistant/partner)
- Emphasizes the cost and timeline estimation feature
- Friendly yet professional
