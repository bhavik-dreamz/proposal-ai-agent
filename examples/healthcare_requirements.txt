Project Name: Healthcare Patient Portal

Project Description:
Develop a secure patient portal that allows patients to access their medical records, schedule appointments, communicate with healthcare providers, and manage prescriptions online.

Key Features:
- Secure patient authentication and authorization
- Electronic health records (EHR) access
- Appointment scheduling and reminders
- Secure messaging with healthcare providers
- Prescription refill requests
- Lab results viewer
- Health data tracking (vitals, medications)
- Mobile app for iOS and Android
- HIPAA compliance

Technology Preferences: HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure, encrypted data storage

Target Audience: Patients of all ages, healthcare providers

Constraints: Must comply with HIPAA regulations, high security requirements
